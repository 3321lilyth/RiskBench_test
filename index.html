RiskBench



Overview

The RiskBench is a scenario-based Risk Assessment Benchmark open to any participant from academia and industry. You only need to sign up on the RiskBench Leaderboard, providing your team name and your institution.

RiskBench is the largest scenario-based benchmark focusing on assessing risks induced by dynamic traffic participants and unexpected events (e.g., collisions and construction zones).We explicitly design a taxonomy of scenarios for scenario generation and scenario-based per-formance analysis. We benchmark four types of existing risk assessment algorithms by assessing them using three risk assessment metrics, i.e., risk identification, risk anticipation, and planning awareness. Refer to this link for detail.
We hope our work can stimulate the community to work collectively in this critical direction to enhance the safety performance of the next-generation intelligent driving systems.
The task of RiskBench is Risk Identification that identifies potential hazards. With variant scenario videos as input, a risk assessment algorithm should be able to identify the locations of risks and anticipate risks, and outputs that if there exist a collision. In this benchmark, we focus on the risks induced by dynamic elements.
